Welcome to MUMC, an outdoors club that has been around since 1944. In this time, we have run thousands of trips, ranging from day walks where you can see amazing landscapes, wildlife and flora — to major adventures on big rivers, deep caves, cliff faces and high mountains that offer rare opportunities to master the demands of such environments and of life.
